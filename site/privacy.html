This policy explains what Daily Log, sold by [Seller legal name], does with your information. The short version: the app has no server and never sends us your data. We only handle the minimum needed to sell you the product and run ads.

1. The app stores your data on your device

Everything you create in Daily Log — habits, tasks, notes, goals — is stored locally in your browser and, if you choose, in a file you save on your own device. It is never transmitted to us. We cannot see it, and we cannot recover it for you. Your backups are yours to keep.

2. Information we do handle

  • Purchase details. When you buy, our payment provider Razorpay collects your name, email, phone number, and payment information to process the transaction. We receive your email and phone number from Razorpay so we can deliver the product and provide support. We do not receive or store your card/UPI details — Razorpay handles those. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Website analytics & advertising. Our website uses the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to measure our Instagram/Facebook ads and understand which visits lead to a purchase. This may set cookies and share limited event data (e.g. page view, checkout started, purchase) with Meta. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Support emails. If you email us, we keep the correspondence to help you.

3. How we use it

  • To deliver your purchase and provide support.
  • To process refunds.
  • To measure and improve our advertising.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Retention

We keep purchase and buyer contact records for as long as needed for support, accounting, and legal/tax obligations. You can ask us to delete your contact details (subject to records we must legally retain).

5. Your choices

  • You can opt out of ad tracking via your browser settings and your Meta ad preferences.
  • You can request access to or deletion of the contact details we hold by emailing [support email].

6. Contact
